## Decisions Made
- **Warm amber accent**: Added as secondary color to break teal monotony -- used for CTAs (newsletter, Build a Brief)
- **Off-white surfaces**: bg-surface (#FAFAF8) replaces pure white for warmth; bg-surface-alt (#F5F3F0) for alternate sections
- **Hamburger menu**: Vanilla JS toggle (not Preact island) to avoid hydration overhead for simple toggle
- **Sticky header**: `sticky top-0 z-40` for persistent navigation
- **Google Fonts**: Inter for Latin, Noto Sans Devanagari for Nepali -- loaded via preconnect for performance
- **Left-aligned hero**: Editorial style over centered hero -- more authoritative for a policy think tank
- **Team photos**: `w-16 h-16 rounded-full object-cover` in horizontal card layout
