Nepal Health Policy Lab is a health policy research initiative dedicated to making rigorous global health evidence accessible and actionable for Nepal's policymakers, practitioners, and public.

We focus exclusively on health. By going deep rather than broad, we aim to become the definitive resource for health policy evidence in Nepal.
