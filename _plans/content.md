# Plan: Content Strategy

## Goal
Establish content infrastructure for blog, digest, and evidence portal.

## Current Status
Not started -- infrastructure being built first.

## Priorities (ordered)

1. Blog content collection with schema validation -- not started
2. Sample blog post to validate pipeline -- not started

4. Evidence portal data structure -- planned (see website.md)

## Content Products

| Product | Audience | Purpose | Cadence |
|---------|----------|---------|---------|
| Blog | Researchers, students | Original findings, personal voice | As needed |
| Digest | Policymakers, practitioners | Curated global evidence | Monthly |
| Evidence Portal | Government, donors, INGOs | Structured synthesis, on-demand | Always-on |

## Decisions & Reasoning

- All blog posts must have named authors
- All content bilingual (English + Nepali)
- Substack for distribution, canonical content on own domain
- Markdown files in repo for now; headless CMS later if needed
